FinX Glass

Operations made coherent.

Glass is the operations surface of the FinX platform. One workspace for customer service, compliance, supervisors, and platform admins — with tasks, queues, and tools organized the way the bank actually works, not the way a vendor's product team happens to ship.

Choose your role → Browse modules

Choose your role

Glass content is organized by what you do, not by where the data lives. Pick the role closest to yours to see the tasks, modules, and permissions tuned for your day.

CSR / Ops Agent

Front-line agents handling customer enquiries, account servicing, and first-touch case work across channels.

  • Look up a customer profile
  • Update contact details
  • Raise a service request
  • Trigger a card reissue
Compliance Officer

Reviews KYC outcomes, AML alerts, sanctions hits, and four-eyes approvals. Owns the regulatory posture of every onboarded customer.

  • Review KYC results
  • Approve high-risk customer
  • Handle AML alerts
  • Sanctions screening
Supervisor

Manages queues, SLA breaches, and team performance. Reassigns work, breaks deadlocks, and signs off on second-line approvals.

  • Monitor queue health
  • Reassign tasks
  • Approve exceptions
  • Review agent performance
Admin / Platform

Configures roles, permissions, workflow definitions, and connector settings. The control plane for how Glass behaves at this bank.

  • Manage RBAC roles
  • Edit workflow definitions
  • Configure connectors
  • Audit access logs

Modules

Six modules make up the Glass workspace. Each is independently navigable; together they cover the full operational surface of a composable bank.

Customer & Account Management
Unified customer 360, account servicing, and arrangement lifecycle across the cores Glue exposes.
Task Management
Personal and team queues, SLA timers, four-eyes hand-offs, and reassignment for every operational task type.
Workflow Console
Visual editor and runtime view for Glue-orchestrated workflows. Inspect a step, retry, or branch from a stuck instance.
Incident & Alert Center
Operational incidents, connector degradations, and AML alerts in one prioritized view with on-call routing.
Service Orchestration Console
Define, deploy, and observe Glue service compositions. Bridges the developer view of Glue with the operator view of Glass.
Fintech Connector SPAs
Embedded operator UIs for Jumio, ComplyAdvantage, and other ecosystem partners — surfaced inside Glass with single sign-on.
